FAQ

Do I have to pay for each translator's account?
No. You can assign translations to chosen translators without paying additional fees for their accounts.
Do I have to pay for each user?
No unless you'e using the free, limited to one user pricing plan.
Are there any additional costs?
Translating on your own is free but extra costs will arise if you order from freelance translators or order managed services, like external project management or DTP.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Text United comes with no contract. Simply opt in for a monthly plan and continue as long as you please.
What's the full-service translation platform?
It's three elements available from your account:
> cloud-based translation software (CAT),
> a marketplace to purchase translations from freelance translators,
> and access to our managed services, if you want Text United to organize your projects.
Can I translate by myself?
Yes. Our translator's tools are available for you at no cost.
